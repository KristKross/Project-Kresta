Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free plan?

Our free plan includes essential features like post creation and basic analytics. It's perfect for individuals just getting started with social media management.

What are the main differences between plans?

The Creator Plan ($9/month) adds unlimited content planning, task management, and team workspace for up to 3 people. The Business Plan ($29/month) includes advanced features, larger team workspace (10 people), and priority support.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be applied at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Yes, we offer custom solutions for larger teams. Contact us to discuss enterprise options with additional features and support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. All plans are billed monthly with no long-term contracts required.

Is there a free trial available?

Our free plan is always available with no time limit. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time to access additional features.
